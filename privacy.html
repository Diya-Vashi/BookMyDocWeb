Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At BookMyDoc, we are committed to protecting your personal health information. We collect information you provide directly to us when registering a clinic, booking an appointment, or interacting with our mobile application.

2. Data Storage and Security

All your information — including appointment records, prescriptions, and medical history — is encrypted and securely stored using industry-standard tools like Firebase Firestore. Our platform uses secure login mechanisms and role-based access to ensure that only authorized users (Doctors, Admins, Staff) can view specific data.

3. AI and Analytics

Any AI-based suggestions provided by BookMyDoc are based on anonymized or permission-granted data only. We do not sell or share your identifiable data with third parties without your explicit consent.

4. Your Rights

You may request to review, modify, or delete your data at any time via the app's settings or by contacting our support team.
